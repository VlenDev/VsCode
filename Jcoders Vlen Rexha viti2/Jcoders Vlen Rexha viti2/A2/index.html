Covid-19 Rules

In response to the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic, our airport has implemented the following safety measures:

  • Face masks are mandatory for all passengers and staff within the airport premises.
  • Temperature checks and health screenings are conducted at entry points.
  • Physical distancing of at least 6 feet is required in all areas.
  • Hand sanitizing stations are available throughout the airport.
  • Travelers are encouraged to check local travel restrictions and guidelines.

Luggage Rules

To ensure a smooth and safe travel experience, please adhere to our luggage rules:

  • Each passenger is allowed one carry-on bag and one checked bag. Additional fees may apply for excess baggage.
  • Carry-on bags must adhere to size and weight restrictions specified by the airline.
  • Prohibited items include sharp objects, flammable materials, and explosives. Please check with your airline for specific guidelines.
  • For fragile items or valuable possessions, consider purchasing additional insurance coverage.
